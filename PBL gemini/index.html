Guidance After 10th

Science

Includes Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and Biology. Ideal for those with a passion for logic, research, and innovation, leading to careers in engineering, medicine, and pure sciences.

Key Skills:

  • Analytical & Problem-Solving
  • Mathematical Aptitude
  • Scientific Research

Industries: Information Technology, Healthcare, R&D, Aviation, Biotechnology.

Avg. Salary Potential: High

Future Scope

AI & Machine Learning, Quantum Computing, Bioinformatics, Sustainable Energy.

Famous Personalities

Sundar Pichai (Engineer), Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (Scientist).

Key Entrance Exams

JEE (Engineering), NEET (Medical), BITSAT.

Commerce

Covers Accountancy, Business, and Economics. Perfect for aspiring entrepreneurs and future leaders in the world of finance, business, and management.

Key Skills:

  • Financial Literacy
  • Business Acumen
  • Data Analysis

Industries: Banking, Consulting, Retail, E-commerce, Insurance.

Avg. Salary Potential: Very Good

Future Scope

FinTech, Digital Marketing, Supply Chain Management, E-commerce Strategy.

Famous Personalities

Warren Buffett (Investor), Indra Nooyi (Business Executive).

Key Entrance Exams

CA Foundation, CUET, NPAT.

Arts & Humanities

Includes History, Arts, and Psychology. Opens doors to a vast range of creative, analytical, and socially impactful careers in law, journalism, design, and public services.

Key Skills:

  • Critical Thinking & Creativity
  • Strong Communication
  • Empathy & Research

Industries: Media, Law, Education, Creative Arts, Public Administration.

Avg. Salary Potential: Good

Future Scope

UX Research, Digital Content Creation, Podcasting, Public Policy Analysis.

Famous Personalities

J.K. Rowling (Author), Steven Spielberg (Filmmaker).

Key Entrance Exams

CLAT (Law), NID (Design), TISSNET.
